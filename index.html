WELCOME TO MOVIE SHARE

Here you can find information about your favorite movies, and provide ratings for each one!

About Us

We are a group of high schoolers in Mr. Millard's AP CSP class. As part of our midterm project, we created a website with HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, and MYSQL to display the most popular movies and show ratings for those movies

How to use this website:

Use the navigation bar to switch between pages. From there, you can view a list of movies and information about them. If you have seen that movie before, you can post a review of your thoughts on the movie.

This project was made by Joel Johnson, Parth Mehta, Matthew Phan, and Sarvesh Chandrakumar for Mr. Millard's AP CSP class.
